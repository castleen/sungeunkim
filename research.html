Research

Research Interests

My research focuses on international political economy, with a particular interest in the intersection of domestic politics and international trade. Key areas include:

Public Attitudes toward Trade Policy Electoral Responses to Trade China's Foreign Economic Strategies Trade & Foreign Aid Policies Political Economy of Environment & Energy
