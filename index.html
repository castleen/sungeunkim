Sung Eun Kim

Associate Professor of Political Science, Korea University

I am an Associate Professor of Political Science at Korea University. I am currently a Visiting Scholar at the Harvard-Yenching Institute (2024–2025). Previously, I was an Assistant Professor at the National University of Singapore.

My research focuses on international political economy, with a particular interest in the intersection of domestic politics and international trade. I study public attitudes toward trade policy, electoral responses to trade, and China's foreign economic strategies.

Research Interests

International Political Economy Trade Policy Domestic Politics & Trade Electoral Responses to Trade China's Foreign Economic Strategies Political Economy of Environment & Energy
