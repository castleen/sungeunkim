I am an Associate Professor at the Department of Political Science and International Relations at Korea University. I am currently a Visiting Scholar at the Harvard-Yenching Institute (2024–2025). Previously, I was an Assistant Professor in Political Science at the National University of Singapore.

My research focuses on international political economy, with a particular interest in the intersection of domestic politics and international trade. I study public attitudes toward trade policy, China's foreign economic strategies, and the political economy of environment and energy.

I received my Ph.D. in Political Science (with Distinction) from Columbia University in 2016.

Contact: sung_kim@korea.ac.kr
